Styling Notes
Split-screen layout: Hero illustration on the left, content on the right.

Subtle animation: .animate-float keyframes on the SVG (you can define in Tailwind config) for gentle up-and-down motion.

Feature grid: Icon + title highlights four core strengths.

Dual CTAs: “Get Started” (primary) and “View Features” (secondary) with distinct styling.

Footer links: Thin text for legal/support links to keep the interface uncluttered.
